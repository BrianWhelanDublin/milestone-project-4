Our Story

Started in Dublin in 2021 Abode.com is an interiors specialist passionate about design and quality. We handpick all items we sell. We work closely with designers to ensure nothing but the best in design and quality for our customers.

Design

At Abode.com we are passionate about design. Our team works very closely with our furniture designers to bring you one off design pieces for your home.

Sustainability

At Abode.com, sustainability is as important to us as the design and quality of our products. We are working closely with our manufactures to ensure all our materials are both ethically sourced and sustainable. We are also working to becoming carbon neutral.
